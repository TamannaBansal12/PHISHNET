Deepfake attacks use AI-generated audio, video, or images to impersonate individuals.

Common deepfake threats:
- Fake CEO voice calls requesting money transfer
- Video impersonation of executives
- AI-generated identity fraud
- Synthetic media used for phishing

Indicators of deepfake:
- Unnatural facial movements
- Lip-sync mismatches
- Inconsistent lighting or shadows
- Robotic or unnatural voice tone
- Audio artifacts or distortion

Prevention:
- Verify identity using multiple channels
- Use biometric verification systems
- Train employees to recognize deepfakes
