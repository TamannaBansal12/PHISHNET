Effective phishing prevention requires both technical and human measures.

Technical controls:
- Use SPF, DKIM, and DMARC for email authentication
- Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Implement URL filtering and sandboxing
- Use email security gateways

User practices:
- Verify sender identity before clicking links
- Avoid entering credentials via email links
- Check domain spelling carefully
- Report suspicious emails immediately

Organizational practices:
- Conduct phishing awareness training
- Monitor email traffic for anomalies
- Maintain updated threat intelligence feeds
