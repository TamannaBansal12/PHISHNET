Social engineering exploits human psychology to bypass security.

Common tactics:
- Urgency ("respond immediately")
- Authority ("this is from your manager")
- Fear ("your account will be blocked")
- Trust ("internal email impersonation")
- Curiosity ("you have received a bonus")

Attackers often combine social engineering with phishing emails, deepfakes, and fake websites.

Defense:
- Awareness training
- Zero-trust mindset
- Verification through official channels
